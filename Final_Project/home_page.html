About Me

Introduction: This map visualizes Indegenous territory, historical sites, and population in the United States. This map is intended to educate individuals on the native populations in the United States. It is important to understand where and how we can support native people.

Data + Methodology: Data is linked in the references page. Reservation polygons were taken from American Conservation and Stewardship Atlas (cleaned in ArcGIS and exported to geojson), historical site locations from google maps with the help of an article published by The Wilderness Society, and all Native population data will be taken from U.S. Census data for 2024 (cleaned in ArcGIS and exported to geojson).

Project summary: Map is not finished, needs some fine tuning and I need to finish the population layer and chart. There has been some difficulty troubleshooting it. These need to be finalized along with final aesthetic details. For final submission, I need to finish the population layer and chart, and make the website more appealing to the eye.
