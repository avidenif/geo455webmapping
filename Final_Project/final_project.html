Boarding Schools Symbols: Layer showing Indigenous boarding schools throughout history in Southwest United States (2024).

  • To implement cultural genocide, between 1869 and the 1960s, children were abducted and brought to these schools miles away from their homes and families. They were abused and mistreated. These schools were operated by federal governments or churches. Today, many of the schools that remain open instead focus on cultural support, community, and celebration.
